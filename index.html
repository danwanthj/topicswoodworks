Our Quality and Attention to Detail

We do not use materials from big box stores as these are not high quality products for our clients.
The wood we use are hand picked form top rated wood mills in rough form and machined meticulously to the final size using jointer, planer, table, saw, wide belt sander, and router.
A lot of time gets put in to each piece to produce the end results. We spend a lot of time to match the patterns when making pieces over 6" wide as we want the results to look natural.
